Budgeting

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan for how to spend your money. This spending plan allows you to determine in advance whether you will have enough money to do the things you need to do or would like to do. It is simply balancing your expenses with your income. Meet Thami and Rapeleng who should have started budgeting a while back and now have a lifestyle they can’t afford. Not to worry, it’s never too late to start.
